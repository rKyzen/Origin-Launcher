# Origin Design System

## Philosophy

Strong defaults over endless customization.

## Experiences

### Origin Pure

Pixel-inspired.
Clean.
Calm.
Familiar.

### Origin Flow

Glass-inspired.
Floating surfaces.
Monochrome-first.
Expressive.

## Rules

- Every setting must justify its existence.
- Avoid exposing implementation details.
- Prefer presets over sliders.
- Prioritize user experience over flexibility.
